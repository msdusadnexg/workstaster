Worktaster

Works..

for Schools & Colleges

Engage your students… build employability… measure progress… hassle-free

Worktaster helps you and your students get the work experience placements they need, and develop employability skills– and it’s free!

Easy Administration, Reporting and Measurement.

Tired of chasing students to complete cv’s, apply for placements and complete paperwork? Employers not filling in all the forms? Admin chasing you for reports, progress and accountability?

WorkTaster handles admin and slashes paperwork with superb progress reports for you, and auto-chase ups for your students. It’s easy to use, a ready-made system and it makes you look great!

Lesson Plans, How To’s and Checklists

Ready to use lesson plans or build your own using the WorkTaster teacher resources based on government recommendations and the CBI employability competencies. See at a glance what needs to be included to meet the latest guidelines. Ready-made plans will help you arrange CV building workshops, engage local employers for practice interviews, give guidance on presentation and more.

Student Support

WorkTaster helps your students build their online profileand CV, search for placements, apply and it tracks their progress. WorkTaster helps them look at career choices, find the right placement for them and improve their chances of getting it. Automated invitations from a CSV file upload of your students helps get them started quickly and easily.

Employer Database

Got your own local employers who already give your students work experience? These placements can be protected so only YOUR students can see them and apply.

Access the WorkTaster database of employers and widen the pool your students can access.

Improve your Careers Education, Information, Advice and Guidance and your OFSTED report.

Use the WorkTaster system to ensure your student's employability skills are developed and the impact is fully evidenced and reported. Used as part of CEIAG, WorkTaster can improve the experience of your students and increase their chances of success.

WorkTaster can help in other ways too....

Want to bring in specialised help? From CV building workshops to practice interview days, whatever you need to help get your students on the road to their work experience placement. Contact WorkTaster today to discuss how we can come into your school or college and work.

Why WorkTaster?

WorkTaster staff have spent the last 18 months in schools, arranging work experience and talking to hard-pressed teachers about the hassles and the time involved. This system has been built around the difficulties teachers face, and aims to bring best practice to every school.
