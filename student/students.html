Why work experience?

You might HAVE to do work experience as part of school or college, or because you are 40% more likely to get a job with work experience. Either way it’s a massive boost when applying for jobs, college places and apprenticeships.

What work experience is good?

Not so sure what career you want? WorkTaster can help you fit your interests and profile to jobs and find a placement to suit you.

Already know what you want to do? - WorkTaster can help you find a placement, and give you the tools you need to be successful .

How do I build a cv or online profile?

Use the WorkTaster profile builder to present yourself at your best. Employers love to see Video and Media – so use them! Upload as part of your profile! Build your WorkTaster profile in easy steps, then convert to CV format at the click of a button. Use WorkTaster to make yourself attractive to employers.

How do I apply for work experience?

Check out local placements and how to apply. Apply directly using your online profile. Track the progress of your applications within the system and accept or decline placements when they are offered.

How do I keep up to date?

WorkTaster Knowledge Base keeps you up to date with the latest news, placements and resources at WorkTaster. Watch a work experience interview, see someone talking about their WorkTaster placement or listen to an employer talking about why work experience makes you more employable. See what’s new on Twitter or look at our latest YouTube videos.
