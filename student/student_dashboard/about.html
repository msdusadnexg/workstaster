Worktaster

WorkTaster is about helping young people reach their full potential. And if we can help a few overworked school careers advisors along the way, all the better. The WorkTaster vision from two friends ran a marathon together in 2013. After struggling to find our own children work experience we began to wonder how difficult it must be to arrange and co-ordinate this process for over 500,000 young people every year!

Since the early days, the idea has morphed hugely as we have seen the challenges faced by schools. WorkTaster evolved into a holistic system to develop the skills and decision-making and motivation of young people.

Sally ran an inner city business for 20 years and has been volunteering in schools around Birmingham to develop work experience.

Claire spent 10 years managing a recruitment business, now runs her own business and also volunteers at local schools to develop work experience.

Jerry has senior digital marketing and general management experience, including new business launches. He is currently Managing Director of Royal London Consumer Insurance.
